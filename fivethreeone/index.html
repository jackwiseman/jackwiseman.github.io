Privacy Policy

BBB Tracker · Effective 18 June 2026

The short version: BBB Tracker collects nothing. There are no accounts, no analytics, no trackers, and no servers. Every workout you log stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no account system and sends no data off your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Data you create in the app

Your training data — maxes, cycles, workout logs, and accessory exercises — is stored only on your device using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). It is never uploaded to us or to any third party.

  • If you have iCloud device backups enabled, this data may be included in your own encrypted Apple backup, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy — not ours.
  • Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party SDKs, advertising, or analytics. It makes no network requests of any kind.

Tracking

We do not track you across apps or websites, and we do not use the device advertising identifier.

Children

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
