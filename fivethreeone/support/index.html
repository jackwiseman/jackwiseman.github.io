Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device. BBB Tracker has no account and no server — nothing is uploaded. See the privacy policy for details.

How do I back up my training history?

Your data is included in your device's standard Apple/iCloud backup if you have backups enabled. Deleting the app removes its data from the device.

How are my weights and progression calculated?

BBB Tracker follows the 5/3/1 program: set your training maxes once and the app prescribes warmups, working sets, the AMRAP top set, and Boring But Big assistance each session. Training maxes increase automatically each cycle (+5 lb upper body, +10 lb lower body).

Something looks wrong with a set or weight.

Email me with your training maxes, the lift, and the week you're on, and I'll take a look.
